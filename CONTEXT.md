## The Vegas Analogy (Core Insight)

Las Vegas is the real-world proof of how new economies evolve from degen playground to mainstream destination.

**The History:**
- 1905: Railroad stop in the desert
- 1931: Hoover Dam workers need entertainment  
- 1940s-60s: The mob builds casinos for degens and gamblers
- 1970s-80s: Corporate ownership, Howard Hughes, legitimization
- 1990s-2000s: THE RESORT IS BUILT ON TOP OF THE CASINO
- Today: $43B economy, mainstream destination for everyone

**The Key Insight:** Vegas built the resort on top of the casino.

The casino is where people speculate (sports books, prediction markets, games of chance). But the RESORT expanded the addressable market because everyone has a different way Vegas can serve them:
- Restaurants and celebrity chefs
- Nightlife and clubs
- Hotels and hospitality
- Shows, residencies, Cirque du Soleil
- Sports - Raiders, Golden Knights, Formula 1
- Shopping and brands
- The Sphere
- Top Golf
- Conventions and conferences

Vegas is now for EVERYONE. The degens are still there, but so is everyone else.

**The Thesis:** The onchain economy is evolving the same way. The casino (speculation, trading, DeFi) came first. Now we build the resort.
